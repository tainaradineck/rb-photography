About me

I am a Brazilian based in Canada, working with photography as a hobby on my spare time. I already lived 6 years in Australia where I discovered my passion about art, video and photography. In addition, I am passionate about music and I am guitarist and lead vocalist at Pink Octopus.

Now living in Vancouver, I am getting inspiration from British Columbia nature, weather and people, that has a big contrast compared to Australia.

Reach me out if you would like to get to know me more.
